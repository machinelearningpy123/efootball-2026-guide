When an older build can make sense

  • Your phone or tablet is on an older Android version and the latest build runs poorly.
  • A recent update changed a feature you used every day.
  • You are testing whether a new problem came from the latest release or from your device setup.

The tradeoff

An older build may restore compatibility, but it can also bring back bugs or miss fixes that matter. If you try one, do it for a clear reason and test the exact feature that pushed you to step back.
